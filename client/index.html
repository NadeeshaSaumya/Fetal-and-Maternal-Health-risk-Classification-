Introduction

Why Fetal/Maternal Health is important?

In the world, health is very important factor to ensure the people lives. Among all categories of people, pregnant women take high priority in health. Because their health depends on two lives. According to the world health organization (WHO), Maternal health refers to the health of women during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postnatal period.

And the fetal health refers to the health of unborn baby(or offspring) develops in the womb (uterus). But monitoring the growth of the fetus and identifying the health status of fetus is very difficult and complicated process. The duration of pregnancy is 9 months, and each three months period of pregnancy is called as trimester. During this each trimester, fetus grows and monitoring the growth is essential factor for fetal health. Therefore, medical checkups and prenatal test are required.

And also, as the reports of world health organization (WHO), approximately 810 women die in each day during the childbirth and with pregnancy related issues in the world when there are preventable situations. Maternal mortality ratio in undeveloped countries is high when comparing with the developed countries. Therefore, fetal and maternal health need more attention in undeveloped and developing countries.

How to monitor the Fetus?

Current technology that mostly used in the world to monitor fetus is cardiotocography. The cardiotocograph (CTG) is a continuous tracing of the fetal heart rate used to assess fetal wellbeing, together with an assessment of uterine activity. That is, mainly it gives the fetal heart rate related information and uterine activity information. This is also called as Electronic Fetal Monitoring (EFM). There is cardiotocographic machine to get cardiotocograph data.

Pregnant woman positioned comfortably in a left lateral or semi-recumbent position to get the CTG data. There are two transducers placed on the abdomen of the pregnant woman and connect those two with a belt. One transducer is pressure sensitive and it correlates with the internal uterine pressure. And this gives the uterine activity information. Other transducer is used to detect fetal heart rate information. That uses ultrasound and the Doppler effect to detect motions.
